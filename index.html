Let's play Ping Pong

Here are the rules of the Game

Ping Pong will count your number from 1 with exception of the following:

  1. Number divisible by 3 become "ping"
  2. Number divisible by 5 become "pong"
  3. Number divisible by both 3 and 5 become "pingpong"
